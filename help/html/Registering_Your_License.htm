Registering Your License

Once PDT is installed, all its features will be available for a 30 day trial. At the end of the 30 day trial, the professional features will be disabled. These include refactoring, getter and setter generation, Zend Framework integration, profiling, PHPUnit testing, debugging through tunneling, advanced code formatting, code galleries, and more.

To enjoy the full range of PDT functionality, you should register a valid license.

To register a valid license and activate all PDT features, go to Help | Register and enter your username or order #, and license key.

 

You can purchase a PDT license from the Zend Store at http://www.zend.com/store/software/studio or from the menu bar go to Help | Purchase a License.

View the Zend Product Online Documentation for the most up-to-date information.
